mail-index

Set up local, private mail search for your AI assistant.

  1. 1. Install prerequisites

    Installs the gog helper and verifies the bundled mail-index CLI.

  2. 2. Sign in with Google

    Opens your browser for read-only Gmail access. Nothing is sent anywhere but your machine.

  3. 3. Sync now

    Indexes the last month of mail locally so it can be searched offline.

  4. 4. Connect to Claude

    Shows the config snippet to paste into your AI assistant.
